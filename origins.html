What is Chapter Surfcraft?

We are a passionate business based in the heart of Europe, Prague. All of our materials are locally grown and responsibly produced in neighboring countries. Each board is lovingly handcrafted by the skilled hands of second-generation sustainable shaper and surfer, Liam Bulgen. Our reputation in the water and the watersports industry continues to grow, reflecting the future of sustainable surf craft.

Located in Prague, CHAPTER is dedicated to crafting sustainable surfboards that redefine the future of wave riding.

Our mission is simple: better boards, better planet.

Who is Liam?

Meet Liam Bulgen, a passionate surf enthusiast from the vibrant city of Cape Town, South Africa. Raised in this coastal paradise, Liam’s love for riding waves started even before he learned to swim.

Fifteen years ago, Liam embarked on an extraordinary journey, dedicating himself to pioneering innovations in surfboard design. Inspired by his father's traditional surfboard workshop, he honed his craft and continually pushed the boundaries of what’s possible.

Liam's mission is to create the ultimate performance surfcraft, specifically tailored to local waves. He is deeply committed to using responsibly sourced materials, ensuring his creations have a minimal ecological footprint.

In his pursuit of sustainability, Liam has forged strong partnerships with organizations like Sustainable Surf and Greenpop. Through these collaborations, all of Liam’s surfboards proudly bear the Ecoboard certification, showcasing his dedication to environmentally friendly practices and a brighter future for our oceans.
