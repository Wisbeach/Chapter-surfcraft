Introducing the Collection

INTRODUCING THE COLLECTION. At CHAPTER, we combine our design expertise and surfing experience with our passion for the environment. Each board is made for a distinct purpose. Our all-water versatile range of surf craft is hand-built from the solid unparalleled foundation of craftsmanship, innovation, and sustainability, naturally setting them apart.

We are a passionate business based in the heart of Europe, Prague. All of our materials are locally grown and responsibly produced in neighboring countries. Every board is handmade by second-generation sustainable shaper and surfer Liam Bulgen. Chapter surf craft represents the next generation of sustainable surf craft with a know reputation in the water and a growing reputation in the watersports industry.
