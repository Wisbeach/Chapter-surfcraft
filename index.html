Introducing the Collection

At CHAPTER, we combine our design expertise and surfing experience with our passion for the environment. Each board is made for a distinct purpose. Our all-water versatile range of surf craft is hand-built from the solid unparalleled foundation of craftsmanship, innovation, and sustainability, naturally setting them apart.

We are a passionate business based in the heart of Europe, Prague. All of our materials are locally grown and responsibly produced in neighboring countries. Each board is lovingly handcrafted by the skilled hands of second-generation sustainable shaper and surfer, Liam Bulgen.
