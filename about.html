Who is Uncle Liam?

Liam z @chaptersurfcraft was born and raised in Cape Town placed in South Africa and started to surf before he could swim. 15 years ago he started to focus on exploring new opportunities and innovation through his personal surfboard designs which he made in father's traditional surfboard workshop. He is on a mission to make the best performance surfcraft for our local wave using responsibly sourced materials. He have partnered with sustainable surf and Greenpop. All his surfcraft will come with Ecoboard certification.
