Who is Uncle Liam?

Meet Liam Bulgen, the surf enthusiast hailing from the vibrant city of Cape Town, South Africa. Born and raised in this coastal paradise, Liam's passion for riding the waves began even before he could swim.

Fifteen years ago, Liam embarked on a remarkable journey, dedicating himself to pioneering innovation and seeking fresh opportunities within the world of surfboard design. Drawing inspiration from his father's traditional surfboard workshop, he honed his craft and pushed the boundaries of what's possible.

Liam is on a noble mission, driven by his commitment to crafting the ultimate performance surfcraft tailored to our local waves. He is unwavering in his dedication to using responsibly sourced materials, ensuring that his creations leave a minimal ecological footprint.

In his pursuit of sustainability, Liam has forged meaningful partnerships with organizations like Sustainable Surf and Greenpop. This collaboration ensures that all of Liam's surfcraft proudly bear the Ecoboard certification, reflecting his commitment to environmentally friendly practices and a brighter future for our oceans.
